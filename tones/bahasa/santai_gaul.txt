## TONE: Santai Gaul (Casual Indonesian)

**Target Audience:** Anak Twitter/Instagram Jakarta, 20-35 tahun urban millennials dan Gen Z

**Voice Description:** Kayak ngobrol sama temen tongkrongan. Natural, relatable, gak dibuat-buat.

---

## LANGUAGE RULES:

### Pronouns:
- Use "gua" dan "lu" (not aku/kamu or saya/anda)
- Exception: Bila perlu soften dikit, bisa mix "gua" dengan "kamu"

### Slang Guidelines:
✅ Allowed & Encouraged:
- "anjir" (shock/emphasis)
- "literally" (untuk emphasis)
- "ngl" / "gak bohong" (not gonna lie)
- "lowkey" / "dikit" (subtle admission)
- "plot twist" (untuk surprise)
- "real talk" (untuk honesty)
- "POV" (point of view setups)
- "vibe" (suasana/feeling)

✅ Natural Indonesian Slang:
- "gila sih" (impressive)
- "parah" (extreme, good or bad)
- "sumpah" (I swear)
- "asli" (really/truly)
- "banget" (very)
- "dong" (persuasive particle)

❌ Don't Force:
- Slang bahasa daerah unless it's super mainstream (misal: "euy" OK, "yah" OK)
- Gen Alpha slang yang terlalu niche
- Trying too hard to be cool

### English Integration:
Mix English naturally for:
- Tech terms: "mindset", "framework", "strategy"
- Common phrases: "by the way", "real talk", "fun fact"
- Emphasis words: "literally", "basically", "honestly"

BUT: Don't overdo it. Masih dominan Bahasa Indonesia.

### Sentence Structure:
- Short sentences. Punchy.
- Kadang one-word sentences. Parah.
- Mix dengan longer sentences untuk variation
- Start dengan "Tapi", "Dan", "Soalnya" = OK and natural
- Sentence fragments untuk emphasis = totally fine

### Grammar "Imperfections":
✅ These make it more human:
- "gak" instead of "tidak"
- "udah" instead of "sudah"
- "emang" instead of "memang"
- Drop "yang" sometimes: "Hal [yang] paling penting..."
- Casual word order

## WRITING EXAMPLES:

### Hook Examples (Slide 1):

**Example 1 - Controversial:**
```
Unpopular opinion: kerja keras itu scam.
```

**Example 2 - Personal Story:**
```
3 tahun lalu gua almost quit.
Best decision I never made.
```

**Example 3 - Relatable:**
```
Dulu gua mikir networking = basa-basi doang.
Salah besar.
```

**Example 4 - Pattern Interrupt:**
```
Gua rugi 50 juta gara-gara satu email.
```

### Body Examples (Slide 2-5):

**Storytelling:**
```
Jadi ceritanya gini.

2020, gua fresh graduate, nganggur, desperate banget cari kerja.

Mass apply ke 100+ companies. Ghosted semua.

Terus gua sadar: approach gua salah dari awal.
```

**Tips/Value Delivery:**
```
Yang gua lakuin:

Stop mass apply. Mulai targeted approach.

Research company dulu. Customize every application.

LinkedIn stalk hiring manager. Kirim personal message.

Sounds like a lot of work? Emang. Tapi ini yang works.
```

**Revelation:**
```
Plot twist: yang bikin gua diterima bukan skill.

It's how I told my story.

Semua orang punya skill. Gak semua orang bisa communicate value mereka.

That's the difference.
```

### CTA Examples (Last Slide):

**Soft CTA:**
```
Save this kalau lu lagi job hunting.

Trust me, you'll need it.
```

**Engagement CTA:**
```
Lu tim "mass apply" atau "targeted approach"?

Jujur aja di comment.
```

**Action CTA:**
```
Try this framework.

Report back hasil lu.

Gua bet 90% yang baca gak akan action. Prove me wrong.
```

## TONE MARKERS:

Must include several of these per post:

- Personal pronouns: "gua", "lu"
- Natural slang: "banget", "parah", "sih"
- English mixing: "literally", "plot twist", "real talk"
- Casual grammar: "gak", "udah", "emang"
- Conversational connectors: "jadi gini", "nah ini dia", "coba dengerin"
- Self-aware humor: "I know, I know...", "Cliché? Maybe."
- Rhetorical questions: "Setuju gak?", "Relate?"

---

## WHAT TO AVOID:

❌ Formal Bahasa:
- "Tidak dapat dipungkiri bahwa..."
- "Pada kesempatan kali ini..."
- "Mari kita bahas..."
- "Dapat disimpulkan bahwa..."
- "Perlu diketahui bahwa..."

❌ Overly Polite:
- "Mohon" (unless actually asking for something serious)
- "Silakan" (too formal)
- "Terima kasih atas perhatiannya" (just say "thanks" or skip it)

❌ Textbook Language:
- Perfect EYD adherence (be natural, not perfect)
- No contractions (use 'em!)
- Complex sentence structures

❌ Try-Hard Cool:
- Forcing every trendy slang
- All-English flex
- Overusing emoji
- Being edgy for no reason

---

## VIBE CHECK:

Read your content out loud.

Does it sound like:
✅ Voice message to a friend
✅ Twitter thread dari mutual
✅ Casual coffee chat

Or does it sound like:
❌ LinkedIn corporate post
❌ School essay
❌ Formal presentation

If it's the second group, rewrite.

## TEMPLATE PATTERNS:

### Pattern 1: Personal Confession
```
Confession: [honest admission]

[Why you thought/did this]

[What changed]

[Lesson/insight]
```

### Pattern 2: Myth Busting
```
Everyone thinks [common belief].

Gua juga dulu gitu.

Ternyata [reality].

Here's why [explanation].
```

### Pattern 3: Story Arc
```
[Specific moment in time]

[The situation]

[What happened]

[The realization]

[The takeaway]
```
